Compound

A crime thriller series pitch for Netflix India

A teenage hacker uncovers a secret vigilante justice network running out of a retired police colony in Prayagraj. What starts as a digital stunt becomes a choice between joining the system—or burning it to the ground.

Download Pitch Deck (PDF)

Main Characters

  • Zaid (15) — gifted hacker from a low-income family.
  • Rhea (17) — activist’s daughter; Zaid’s moral compass.
  • ACP Sinha (60s) — retired legend, mastermind of the network.
  • Bunty (30s) — compound fixer; witness-protected ex-hitman.
  • Judge Subhash (70s) — fading memory; holds buried truths.

Season 1 Snapshot

  • Ep1: Zaid hacks the police grid; finds a deleted execution clip.
  • Ep2–4: Ledgers, cover-ups, rising danger—Zaid becomes a target.
  • Ep5: Origin of the compound’s doctrine of “justice.”
  • Ep6–8: Leak, ultimatum, betrayal—burn the compound.
